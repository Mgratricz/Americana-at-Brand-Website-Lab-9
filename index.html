Shopping

You will find a variety of brands from global brands to local surprises. Iconic fashion stores like Brandy Melville and Nordstrom can be found and more. YOu will also see Barnes and Nobles, a local favorite bookstore fill the plaza as its building rises over everything.

Dining

No shopping complex is complete without its collection of dining services. From popular dessert locations to every meal of the day, the American will contain everything you crave. You will see a multi cultural food choice with Din Tai Fung and Frida's Mexican Cuisine while iconic snack locations like Potato Corner and Sprinkles can also be found.

Atmosphere

One of the most exciting features of this shopping complex is the feeling of the place. The Americana gives off this vintage and retro vibe. From the architecture to the music and to the decorations. It will make you feel like you are transported to another place.

About

The Americana is a great place to for entertainment, to relax, shop and more. The feeling of the place makes you feel alive and you will see it in people's faces. Whether it is with your family or your friends, you will always find something to do. The AMC Theatres will always be open for any movie goers while the grass area is a great place to relax and take a nap.
